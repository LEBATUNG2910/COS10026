Position 1: IT Project Manager

Position Title: IT Project Manager

Brief Description: We are seeking an experienced IT Project Manager
to lead and oversee our technology projects from inception to completion.

Salary Range: $80,000 - $110,000 per year

Reports to: Director of Technology

Key Responsibilities:

    Conceptualize and design cutting-edge VR applications and simulations.

    Collaborate with cross-functional teams to bring VR projects to life.

    Stay up-to-date with the latest VR technologies and trends.

    Create and optimize 3D models and environments for VR experiences.

    Ensure VR projects are visually stunning and user-friendly.

Required Qualifications, Skills,
Knowledge, and Attributes:

Essential:

    Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field.

    Strong proficiency in 3D modeling and design software (e.g., Unity, Unreal Engine).

    Experience in VR development (2+ years).

    Excellent problem-solving and creative thinking skills.

Preferable:

    Master's degree in Virtual Reality or related field.

    Published VR projects or applications.

    Experience with haptic feedback and VR hardware.
